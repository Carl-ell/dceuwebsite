BATMAN V SUPERMAN

It's been nearly two years since Superman's (Henry Cavil) colossal battle with Zod (Michael Shanon) devastated the city of Metropolis.
The loss of life and collateral damage left many feeling angry and helpless, including crime-fighting billionaire Bruce Wayne (Ben Affleck).
Convinced that Superman is now a threat to humanity, Batman embarks on a personal vendetta to end his reign on Earth, while the conniving
Luthor (Jesse Eisenberg) launches his own crusade against the Man of Steel.
